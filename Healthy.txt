Description:
Healthy lettuce plants have vibrant, crisp, and well-formed leaves. They exhibit a rich green color, with no signs of wilting, discoloration, or spots. The plants are sturdy and grow well, with a robust root system that supports their overall vitality.

Cause:
Healthy lettuce is the result of optimal growing conditions, including appropriate soil, water, and nutrient management. Factors such as adequate sunlight, proper spacing, and the absence of pests or diseases contribute to its health.

Remedy:
To maintain healthy lettuce, ensure that you provide:

Well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
Regular watering, keeping the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
Adequate sunlight (at least 6 hours a day).
Proper spacing to allow good air circulation.
Monitoring for and promptly addressing any signs of pests or diseases.
